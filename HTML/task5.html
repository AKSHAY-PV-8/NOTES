Profile

Lionel Messi
From Argentina; widely considered one of the greatest of all time. Known for his dribbling, vision, goal-scoring and longevity at the top level. Has won numerous club & individual honours (Champions League, Ballon d’Or, World Cup).
Cristiano Ronaldo
Portuguese forward famed for his athleticism, goal-scoring consistency, and versatility in attack. He excels in aerial duels, speed, power and has broken many scoring records across different leagues. Has played for top clubs in different countries, achieving success internationally and at club level.
